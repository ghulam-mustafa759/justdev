Just And Humane Development

JUST AND HUMANE DEVELOPMENT seeks to promote justice in the sphere of development and synergises the expertise, experience, & skillset of academics, development professionals, social science researchers, data scientists & policy analysts. It provides consultancy services to Government, Multilateral institutions, Non Profit Organizations and Businesses. It is registered with the Security and Exchange Commission of Pakistan, as a Private Limited Company with offices in Islamabad and Multan.
