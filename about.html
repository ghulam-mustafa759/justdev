About Us

DEVELOPMENT envisions a “Just inclusive Society benefiting all people”. Its Mission is of “Working with partner communities & like-minded organizations, individuals to implement provisions of Article 38-d for ensuring basic necessities of life to the citizens of Pakistan.

It is the brainchild of Doctor Sarfraz Ansari, a lifelong academic specializing in Development Planning, Management, Political Science and Sociology. Dr. Sarfraz is also the driving force behind Doaba Foundation, where he has facilitated the development and implementation of an “Inclusive model of self-reliance and empowerment of local communities based on respect of local wisdom and sustainable utilization of local resources”
